PROGRESS

OCT 2018

×

Characterized the poem "Progress" by Darison
Strange through a typography study. The poem
addresses the tediousness of making progress,
how every moment and decision in life can either
make us see or question our progress. The
descending stair-like quality along with various
moods set by different font styles help to
convey this pessimistic view of progress.

GOOD FOOD
MAY 2018
×

A multi-use building dedicated to exposing the community
to food endeavors through the concept of collaging program.
The juxtaposition between different, adjacent spaces provides
opportunities for new spaces and typologies. The library
section of the building is a primary activator that is distributed
throughout to juxtapose with other program. This dynamic
system exposes users to learn about food production and
consumption by redefining the library as an educating space
without the dependency on books.
